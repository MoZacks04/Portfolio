About

I like systems that are measurable, verifiable, and shippable. My work spans FPGA prototyping, RTL verification, and transistor-level design.

  • Sim/Debug: Verilator, GTKWave
  • VLSI: Cadence Virtuoso (130nm), DRC/LVS
